ṢAD GOSVĀMĪ

Documentary series that explores in detail the biographies, teachings, philosophy, and literary heritage of the Goswamis of Vrindavan.

 Our goal is to present the life stories of these great saints and their relationships with Lord Sri Chaitanya Mahaprabhu in a way that is both authoritative and captivating.

The film is based on factual accounts from respected Gaudiya Vaishnava texts, such as Sri Chaitanya Charitamrita, Bhakti Ratnakara, Bhakta Mala, Sadhana Dipika, Prema Vilasa, and others.
