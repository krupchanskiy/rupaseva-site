Значение

Sri / श्री / BEAUTY

The land and structures, lectures and kirtans, service and relationships – everything should reflect the original beauty, the beauty of the spiritual world. This beauty is the essence and criterion of everything we do.

RUPA / रूप / FORM

Rupa is the embodiment of bhakti, the object of our service. It represents our acharyas – Rupa Goswami and Srila Prabhupada. It encompasses the limbs of bhakti, primarily śravanam and kirtanam. And of course, it also refers to our magnificent Sri Sri Radha Govinda.

SEVA / सेवा / SERVICE

Both the perfection of life and the means to get closer to it. Everything we do in our ashram occurs in the spirit of service.

KUNJ / कुञ्ज / KUNJ

A secluded sacred place, sheltered from the surrounding hustle and curious eyes. A place where one can fully immerse in a simple and beautiful spiritual life.
